"Flamird" Privacy policy

1.Scope of application

When you use this software network service, this software automatically receives and records the information on your mobile phone, including but not limited to the data such as the start-up of this software, the frequency of the use of functions, etc.

2.Use of information

After obtaining your data, the software will upload it to the server for the author to analyze the use of the software, so as to provide you with better services.

3.Information disclosure

This software will not leak the collected information to any third party.

4.Information Storage and Exchange

The information and data collected by this software will be saved on the server of this software. The information is private access, and only the author can view it only.

5.Information safety

This software uses passkeys to log in. No sensitive data were collected. There is no obvious risk of information security.
